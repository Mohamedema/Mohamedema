### 🔬 About Me
I am a PhD student and Researcher at the **Interdisciplinary Centre of Marine and Environmental Research (CIIMAR), University of Porto**.

**Single-Cell Transcriptomics:** High-dimensional data analysis and clustering for disease modeling.
